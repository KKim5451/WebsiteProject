Cat Informations

Our cats are very interactive with the customers, they will often times put their head against you and rub it. This shows that the cats have interest in, so you'd better pat them if you like em' back! Due to the possible hairs floating into your drinks, we wash them couple times a week and brush them every morning in order to reduce the possibility of this occasion. Our cats are owner-fed, meaning the customers should not give any treats to them in case of the food being posionous to the cats and possibly risk their lives.

Fun fact: Cats are from the same family as the lions are, cats are just smaller versions of lions!
